## About

AIxGRC is a personal brand and knowledge portfolio focused on the responsible development, deployment, and governance of artificial intelligence. It was created to bridge the gap between fast-moving AI innovation and the compliance, risk, and policy frameworks that organizations need to adopt it safely.

This site serves as a living portfolio — housing original research, whitepapers, thought leadership, and project work developed through academic study at Georgetown University and independent professional development.
